Our Story

Andrea is named after a baby who survived because help arrived in time.

We built this platform between two dates we'll never forget — a loss, and a life. Same family. Two different outcomes, decided by whether funds could be raised fast enough.

2023 The loss

We lost our father.

Anaemia and heart failure. We couldn't raise the funds fast enough, and we couldn't trust where they'd go. The system failed a family that did everything right.

2025 The life

Andrea was born premature.

Three months of ICU care. This time we raised the funds in time — and she survived. That's the mission, made literal: nobody should beg blindly, and no patient should be denied care because help arrived too late.
